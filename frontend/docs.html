🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to Burme Transcript! This guide will help you get started with creating and editing subtitles for your videos.

1

Upload a Video

Click on the video upload zone or drag & drop your MP4, WebM, or MOV file to get started.

2

Upload or Create SRT

Upload an existing SRT subtitle file or create new subtitles manually.

3

Edit & Adjust

Edit subtitle text, adjust timestamps, and apply styles to match your video.

4

Export

Export your subtitles as SRT, JSON, or TXT files for use in video players.

🌐 Translation

Use our AI-powered translation feature to translate subtitles to different languages.

1

Go to Translate Page

Click the 🌐 button in the header or navigate to translate.html

2

Upload Files

Upload both your video and SRT subtitle file.

3

Select Languages

Choose source and target languages from the dropdown menus.

4

Choose API

Select Google Gemini or Groq (Llama) for translation and enter your API key.

5

Translate

Click "Translate Subtitles" and wait for the translation to complete.

💡 Pro Tips

💡 Pro Tips
  • Use keyboard shortcuts: Space to play/pause, Arrow keys to seek
  • Adjust time offset to sync subtitles with video
  • Split long subtitles into shorter sections for better readability
  • Use the style editor to customize subtitle appearance
  • Export in multiple formats for different use cases
